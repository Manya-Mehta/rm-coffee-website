THE HISTORY OF COFFEE - FROM BEAN TO BREW

The history of coffee is as rich and complex as the beverage itself. What began as a mystical berry in Ethiopia has evolved into a global phenomenon, shaping societies and cultures around the world. From ancient coffeehouses to modern cafés, coffee continues to be a symbol of conversation, creativity, and connection. Whether enjoyed as a simple black cup or an elaborate latte, coffee’s journey from bean to brew has left a lasting impact on our global culture.

HEALTH BENEFITS OF COFFEE: MORE THAN JUST A MORNING BOOST

Coffee isn’t just a delicious way to start the day — it’s also loaded with health benefits that can improve physical performance, enhance brain function, and protect against chronic diseases. With its antioxidants, nutrients, and caffeine content, coffee has proven to be more than just an energy booster. Whether you prefer a simple black cup or an elaborate latte, you can feel good about the many ways coffee is contributing to your overall well-being.
